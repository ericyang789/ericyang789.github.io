Hello! I am an undergraduate student in my final quarter completing my BS in Bioengineering and minor in applied mathematics at the University of Washington. Since my first introduction to computer science, I have been fascinated by the tremendous impact that data and computational tools have in driving medical innovation. With this passion and curiosity, I continue to apply biomedical informatics principles in my research in tissue engineering, global health and genomics in both academic and industry settings. Specifically, I am interested in leveraging machine learning tools to advance personalized medicine and progress genomic-based disease diagnosis and treatment. This fall, I will be attending the Master of Biomedical Informatics program at Harvard Medical School to further my academic training.

In my courses, research, internship experiences and spare time, I have completed various projects that have allowed me to develop my expertise in software development and engineering design.
