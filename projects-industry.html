Continuous Genomic Sequencing

Summer 2019

I served as a research associate intern within the materials science and advanced platform technology development team at Illumina, Inc, a world leader in sequencing. To reduce sequencing waste and decrease genomic sequencing turn-around time, I developed and automated surface-based DNA assays that enables continuous sequencing on a single surface. Throughout the project, I designed quality control assessments and analyzed next generation sequencing data with in-house software and ImageJ. In addition, I presented research results to senior scientists, managers and directors to support patent applications and product development in the company. Although I can not disclose many details of the project due to NDA commitments, I am happy to share my amazing Illumina experience with anyone who is interested.
