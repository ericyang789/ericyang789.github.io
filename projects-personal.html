Covid-19 Testing Triage

Spring 2020

During the Covid-19 outbreak in 2020, I participated in the MIT Beat the Pandemic Hackathon. My interdiciplinary team brainstormed and prototyped a mobile application to identify essential workers likely to transmit the virus, enabling the ability to triage limited testing resources. The technology computed risk scores based on self reporting and consented GPS-based user data. I was tasked to showcase the frontend interface for clinician and patient ease-of-use using Figma. The team presented the proposed solution to healthcare and technology leaders, demonstrating the potential for this triaging technology.
